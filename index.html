About Me

Welcome to my academic portfolio! I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at University Name. My research focuses on artificial intelligence, machine learning, and their applications in healthcare.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University in 2015, where I worked on developing novel algorithms for medical image analysis. Before that, I completed my Master's degree at MIT and my Bachelor's at University of California, Berkeley.

Currently, I lead the AI for Healthcare Lab, where we explore innovative ways to apply machine learning techniques to solve critical healthcare challenges. Our recent work has been published in top-tier venues such as NeurIPS, ICML, and Nature Medicine.

I am passionate about mentoring students and fostering an inclusive research environment. I am actively looking for motivated graduate students interested in AI and healthcare to join my research group.
